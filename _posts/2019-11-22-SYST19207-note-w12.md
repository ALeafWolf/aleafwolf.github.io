# ARM (Advanced RISC Machine)
## RISC (load and store)
* one cycle per CPI
* used for mobile design
## Advantages:
* Very good power efficiency, less heat
* Lower transistor count
* simpler instructions than Intel

## ARMv7-A
* 32-bit
* support full hardware floating point
* advanced SIMD support

## Coprocessors
* 16 coprocessors (cp)
